Frequently Asked

How current is the merchant pricing data?
Validation sweeps run daily across active merchant frameworks, so baseline procurement receipts reflect live, localized rates rather than outdated national average cost books.
Which drawing formats can Arya Estimating ingest?
Vector PDFs, DWG/DXF CAD exports, and NBS-structured specification documents. Structural parsing isolates volumes and material grades automatically.
Is my project data kept confidential?
All drawings and ledgers are encrypted in transit and at rest. Your project data is never used to price competitors' tenders, and enterprise tiers add SSO and full audit controls.
Do you cover regions outside Leinster?
The national merchant index on Consultancy and Enterprise tiers extends coverage across all Irish provinces, with bespoke regional integrations available on request.
