AWDS Management Team — Roles & ResponsibilitiesManagement structure with core responsibilities by role
AWDS Project Director
  • Overall project leadership, strategy and key decision-making
  • Ultimate accountability for safety, quality, programme & budget
  • Senior client / TII relationship and project governance
  • Chairs project board; approves major change and key risk
AWDS Project Management Lead
  • Day-to-day leadership of delivery across all workstreams
  • Owns programme, resourcing, risk, change and reporting
  • Single point of accountability for delivery performance
  • Leads integration and coordination across the team
AWDS Deputy Project Manager
  • Deputises for the PM Lead and supports daily delivery
  • Coordinates operations, team interfaces and milestones
  • Drives consistent process and progress tracking
  • Supports stakeholder and interface management
AWDS Design Management Lead
  • Leads and integrates multidisciplinary design development
  • Owns design programme, interfaces and technical integration
  • Responsible for design quality, assurance and approvals
  • Manages the design reachback team and design change
AWDS PMO Lead
  • Establishes PMO governance, systems and project controls
  • Owns reporting, KPIs, baseline and information management
  • Coordinates finance, document control and assurance
  • Maintains programme baseline, dashboards and KPIs
AWDS Technical Delivery Lead
  • Ensures technical compliance and verification across contracts
  • Manages assurance processes and technical documentation
  • Upholds engineering standards and resolves technical issues
  • Manages the design-to-construction technical interface
AWDS Procurement Lead
  • Leads procurement strategy and supply-chain engagement
  • Manages tendering, evaluation and contract award
  • Ensures procurement compliance and governance
  • Coordinates the procurement support team and schedules
AWDS Commercial Lead
  • Owns commercial strategy, cost control and change management
  • Manages NEC contract administration and commercial risk
  • Leads commercial reporting and value management
  • Oversees estimating, project controls and value engineering
AWDS Cost Management Lead
  • Manages cost planning, estimating and forecasting
  • Controls budgets, cash flow and cost reporting
  • Supports change valuation and commercial assurance
  • Maintains cost data, benchmarking and forecast accuracy
